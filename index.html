Good to know

The honest bits.

First meeting

Hanji doesn't love a big, enthusiastic hello at first meets. Give her space at first and let her come to you — she warms up and gets comfortable with rough play and cuddles before long.

Alone time

She cries when left alone or separated from her persons. She settles in over time, but does best with company or a patient, gradual adjustment.

Territorial streak

Can get territorial in new situations — not aggressive, just vocal and opinionated. She's playful at heart!

Energy levels

She's a lot of dog sometimes. An active household or a solid exercise routine keeps her settled and happy.
